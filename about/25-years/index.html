Our Story

A Quarter Century of Securing India

In 2001, India was just beginning its digital transformation. Enterprise networks were expanding, e-commerce was taking its first steps, and the threat landscape was nascent but growing. It was in this environment that Network Intelligence was born — with one mission: to make India's digital infrastructure safe.

Twenty-five years later, we've protected India's largest banks, hospitals, critical infrastructure, and growing enterprises through every major cyber threat era — from early network intrusions to ransomware, from cloud security to the AI threat landscape of today.

And in 2026, we're taking our biggest leap yet: launching Transilience — our AI-native security platform that combines everything we've learned over 25 years into products that protect your AI era.

25th Anniversary Message: "25 years ago, we made a promise to secure India's digital future. Today, we renew that promise for the AI era — with smarter tools, deeper expertise, and an unwavering commitment to trust." — NI Leadership
Timeline

Key Milestones

2001

Founded in Mumbai

Network Intelligence established with a focus on network security consulting for Indian enterprises. First office in Mumbai.

2004

First Managed SOC

Launched India's first fully managed Security Operations Center, offering 24/7 monitoring when the concept was still novel in India.

2008

CERT-In Empanelment

Received CERT-In empanelment, establishing NI as a trusted partner for India's critical infrastructure security.

2012

OT Security Practice

Pioneered operational technology (OT/SCADA) security in India, protecting critical sectors like power, water, and manufacturing.

2016

ISO 27001 & PCI DSS Leadership

Became a leading implementation partner for ISO 27001 and PCI DSS for India's banking and financial services sector.

2019

Cloud Security Practice

Launched dedicated cloud security practice as Indian enterprises accelerated their move to AWS, Azure, and GCP.

2022

AI Security Research

Established AI Security Research Lab — one of the first in India — focused on threats to and from AI systems.

2024

Transilience R&D Begins

Began building Transilience — applying 23 years of threat intelligence into AI-native security products.

2026 🎉

25th Anniversary & Transilience Launch

Celebrating 25 years with the launch of Transilience — Rainier, Kamet, and Shasta — NI's AI-native security platform for the next 25 years.
