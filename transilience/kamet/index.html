FAQ

Common Questions

Will Kamet disrupt our production systems? +
No. Kamet uses non-destructive testing techniques and works within agreed scope boundaries. All testing parameters are defined upfront and production-safe modes are available. For OT environments, we have specialized non-intrusive testing protocols.
How is Kamet different from vulnerability scanning (like Nessus, Qualys)? +
Vulnerability scanners identify potential weaknesses. Kamet actually exploits them (safely) to confirm they're real and show their business impact. A scanner might flag 1,000 issues — Kamet will tell you which 30 are actually exploitable and how an attacker would use them.
Do we still need manual pen-testing with Kamet? +
Kamet significantly reduces your dependency on annual manual pen-tests by providing continuous coverage. We recommend a hybrid approach: Kamet for continuous coverage plus NI's expert red team for complex scenarios like social engineering, physical access, and advanced persistent threat simulations.
How does Kamet help with compliance? +
Kamet provides automated evidence for penetration testing requirements under PCI DSS, ISO 27001, RBI Master Directions, SEBI CSCRF, and DPDP Act. Reports are formatted to meet auditor requirements.
