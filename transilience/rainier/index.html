FAQ

Common Questions

What is Rainier and how does it differ from a traditional MSSP? +
Rainier is an AI-native SOC product — not just a managed service. Traditional MSSPs rely on human analysts reviewing alerts. Rainier's AI processes millions of events in real-time, triaging and correlating before human eyes even see it. This means faster detection, fewer false positives, and more meaningful analyst work.
How long does Rainier take to deploy? +
Initial deployment and data ingestion takes 5–10 business days. Full AI tuning and optimized detection coverage typically takes 30 days. NI's team handles the entire setup process.
Does Rainier work with my existing SIEM? +
Yes. Rainier integrates with all major SIEM platforms including Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, IBM QRadar, and Google SecOps. It enhances your existing investment rather than replacing it.
What compliance frameworks does Rainier support? +
Rainier supports automated reporting for ISO 27001, PCI DSS, DPDP Act, RBI Cybersecurity Framework, SEBI CSCRF, HIPAA, and SOC 2. Custom framework mapping is available.
Is my data secure with Rainier? +
Rainier is available as a cloud-hosted or on-premises deployment. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. NI is ISO 27001 certified and CERT-In empanelled. India-resident data options are available for DPDP compliance.
